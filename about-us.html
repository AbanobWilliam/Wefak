About us:

AlWefak Al-Saudi for food industries in Egypt was established in 2008. AlWefak Al-Saudi has expertise of more than 25 years in food industries, where the Egyptian “DoniaElAsal Company” and the Saudi “WadiAlNahilCompany” were mergedin Egypt at El Obour Cityon more than 5,000 square meters, in a factory comprises the latest technology production lines and quality control, and a remarkable blend that combines experience and youth labors.Since the companyis concerned abouthuman resources of the best expertise in the field of production and quality management, the company is also concerned about the training of young personnel, in addition to providing distinctive medical care. The outstanding experience shared, between both honored companies in the field of food production and distribution, is a new shift in this area in Middle East.Al Wefak Company is proud to obtain 4 ISO certificates, which put it in the rank of the exporter companies throughout the world.
